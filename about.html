About the Author

Hi!

My name is Zach Grabowski, and I am an ethusiast for all things sports. Hockey and football tend to be my favorite, but all are welcome here on this blog!

My bio includes knowing most things about football and a love for the game of hockey. I still play beer league hockey with all of my buddies to this day. We all keep up and stay in touch one night a week, when we come together for some awful hockey being played between all of us. Most of the time it ends in a loss, but hey at least there is beer for after the game. I started playing hockey when I was 6, out on our backyard rink. Every year my dad made a small rink in our side yard and he always kept up on it. I skated every year until I started playing organized hockey when I was around 8 years old. Ever since then, I have fell in love with the game.

In my free time I tend to spend a lot of time on the golf course. Whether it is practicing or playing, I always love being on the course with friends and family enjoying our time together. I also love to fish and be outside. It is my dad and I who tend to fish a lot together, especially for salmon here in Michigan, but also go out with friends who enjoy fishing juts like me. If you have any of the same interests, whether it is sports, fishing, golfing, etc. then you are in the right place!
